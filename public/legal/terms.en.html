Terms of Use (English summary)

This page is a convenience summary in English. Until region-specific counsel review, the Korean Terms control.

Platform role

WakeAgain (operated by CoreLabs) is an intermediary marketplace for dormant digital projects. We are not a party to user-to-user transactions and do not guarantee quality, escrow completion, or asset transfer until payment rails (PG) and region rules are fully configured.

Fees

Listing is free. On a successful sale, the seller pays a platform fee (currently 10% of the sale price), as described in the Korean terms (Article 13).

Accounts & age

Korea-facing operations require users to be at least 14. Global regions may set a higher gate (e.g. 16) via product config. Identity levels (email → profile → settlement) gate listing and deal completion.

Currency

Amounts are stored and settled in KRW until multi-currency payment is launched. USD/EUR figures in the UI are display conversions only.
